Fast. Functional. Fun.

TCLB is a free and open source CFD solver based on Lattice Boltzmann Method with a wide range of applicability, including complex fluid flows involving turbulence and heat transfer, electrokinetics, multiphase problems, and non-newtonian fluids.

High performance on GPU

Very good scalability of the TCLB code gives the posibility Its core is a high-performance CUDA code, which can be ran efficiently on multiple GPUs, with performance far beyond usual, CPU-based programs.
