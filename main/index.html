TCLB is a free, open source, CFD solver based on Lattice Boltzmann Method with a wide range of applicability, including complex fluid flows involving turbulence and heat transfer, electrokinetics, multiphase problems, and non-newtonian fluids.
Its core is a high-performance CUDA code, which can be ran efficiently on multiple GPUs, with performance far beyond usual, CPU-based programs.
Adjoint capability can also be found in TCLB, and can be used for topology optimization.
